# Femn  
**Empowering Women & Marginalized Communities**

Femn is a holistic digital ecosystem designed to **support, educate, connect, and empower women and marginalized communities**. By integrating community support, safety tools, educational resources, and activist channels, Femn bridges digital connection with real-world safety and collective impact.

---

## üåü Overview

Femn is more than a social app. It is a **multifaceted platform** built to address personal growth, safety, expression, and activism in one cohesive, user-centered environment.

From temporary **Spark Groups** for candid discussions to a crowd-sourced **Safety Heatmap**, Femn enables users to connect meaningfully while staying protected and informed.
